Welcome to Elyssa's Website.

I'm Elyssa! I live in Green Mountain Falls, a very small town that made my dream of mountain living a reality last year.
I am a Physical Geography and GIS student, and it's looking good for me to graduate in May 2022.
I am a B-licensed skydiver, so when I'm not studying maps and earth forms, you can find me falling out of planes. So far I have 135 jumps over 6 different drop zones. On top of that, I am a bartender 5 days a week at a small mountain bar that I love. While I love the hustle of being busy, at the end of the day my favorite things in life are my dog, my partner and homemade tacos, in that order.

Here are the dropzones I have visited so far:

  • Out of the Blue (Limon, CO)
  • Mile Hi Skydiving Center (Longmont, CO)
  • Skydive Elsinore (Elsinore, CO)
  • Skydive Arizona (Eloy, AZ)
  • Chicagoland Skydive Center (Rochelle, IL)
  • Meadow Peak Skydiving (Marion, MT)
  • Skydive Kansas City (Butler, MO)
